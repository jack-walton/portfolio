# Buy Tickets

Complete the following steps to buy tickets.

<Steps>

## Select Offer

After you [search for an event](/AXS/search-for-events), tap **Select Offer**. Select either **Tickets** or, if available, **AXS Official Resale** to buy tickets from another user. Select the number of tickets that you want. Then, tap **Continue**. Select your preferred delivery method, such as mobile delivery or print at home, then tap **Continue**.

![The offer selection screen with Tickets and AXS Official Resale options](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_1.jpg)

## Enter Your Payment Information
    
Enter your credit card information, or select another available payment method. Optionally, select the checkbox to save this payment method for future purchases. 

![The payment information entry form](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_6.jpg)

When you have entered all your payment information, tap **Continue**.

## Complete Purchase

Enter your billing address, then tap **Continue**. Select whether you want to add ticket insurance to protect your purchase if your plans change. Review your order summary, including ticket quantities, prices, and fees, as well as the **Terms of Use**.

![The order review screen with a Confirm Purchase button](/images/AXS/buy_tickets/buy_tickets_step_10.jpg)

If you agree to the terms, select the checkbox and tap **Confirm Purchase**.

</Steps>
